Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?


We provide comprehensive event and exhibit solutions, covering every aspect of booth fabrication and setup. Our services include booth design & consultation to bring your vision to life, booth fabrication & setup for high-quality execution, provincial setups to accommodate events across different locations, overall booth management to ensure a seamless event experience, furniture and tradeshow equipment rental to complete your setup, and booth games & activities to engage visitors and enhance interaction.

Do you handle booth fabrication outside Metro Manila?


Yes! We extend our services beyond Metro Manila to key areas such as the Davao Region, Cagayan de Oro, and Cebu City, Philippines. Our team ensures smooth logistics and high-quality booth fabrication regardless of location, so you can have a well-executed booth setup wherever your event takes place.

Can you customize booths based on our brand and event needs?


Absolutely! We specialize in custom booth design and fabrication tailored to reflect your brand identity, event goals, and spatial requirements. Whether you need a minimalist, high-tech, or interactive booth, our team collaborates with you to create a unique and engaging exhibit that enhances your brand presence.

Do you offer booth equipment and furniture rentals?


Yes! We provide a wide range of rental options, including tables, chairs, display shelves, LED screens, kiosks, and audio-visual equipment. Our furniture and booth equipment rentals ensure that your exhibit space is not only functional but also visually appealing and comfortable for visitors.

Can you manage the entire booth operation during the event?


Yes! Our booth management service ensures a smooth and worry-free event for you. We provide on-site staff for setup, maintenance, troubleshooting, and real-time assistance. After the event, we also handle the dismantling and storage of your booth, allowing you to focus on engaging with your audience.

Do you provide interactive booth activities or games?


Yes! We offer a variety of interactive booth experiences designed to attract visitors and increase engagement. Our activities include spin-the-wheel games, photo booths, digital activations, product demonstrations, and lead generation activities. These elements help create a fun and memorable experience for attendees.
